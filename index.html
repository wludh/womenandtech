What is an ePortfolio?

According to Clemson University an ePortfolio is a collection of work in an electronic format that showcases learning over time. They are also wonderful tools to showcase your skillset and abilities to employers or grad schools as you move on from W&L.

ePortolios are one of the best ways to display your work. Since they are online, they can support almost any different media format. Whether you are in the C-School, Journalism and Mass Comm., or a more traditional liberal arts major, an ePortfolio will help you become more marketable. It is also a great tool for self-reflection and learning, as you look back and collect the most important works you have created during your time at W&L.
